Frequently Asked Questions

How does the image enhancement work?

Our image enhancement technology uses advanced AI algorithms to analyze your images and improve various aspects such as resolution, lighting, color balance, and detail sharpness. The process is fully automated and takes just seconds to complete.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take data security very seriously. Your images are processed securely and are not shared with third parties. We also offer the option to delete your images from our servers after processing.

What file formats are supported?

We currently support JPG, JPEG, PNG, and WEBP file formats. We're working on adding support for more formats in the future.

How can I get help if I have issues?

You can reach our support team via email at support@imageenhancer.com or by filling out the contact form on this page. We typically respond within 24 hours.
